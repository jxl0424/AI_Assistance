# Jarvis Capabilities

Jarvis is a multi-modal AI assistant designed to help you with daily tasks, productivity, and information retrieval.

## 1. Core Interaction
- **Wake Word**: Say **"Jarvis"** or **"Hey Jarvis"** to activate.
- **Voice Interaction**: Speak naturally; Jarvis understands context and intent.
- **HUD Interface**: A visual "Iron Man" style interface that shows system stats, transcripts, and visualizers.

## 2. Productivity & Organization
- **Smart Startup**: Summarizes your day's tasks and provides advice when you start the system.
- **Reminders**:
  - "Remind me to call Mom at 5 PM"
  - "Remind me to take meds every day at 9 AM"
- **Timers**:
  - "Set a timer for 15 minutes"
  - "Timer for pasta for 10 minutes"
- **Task Management**:
  - "What are my reminders?"
  - "Do I have anything due today?"

## 3. Information & Knowledge
- **Web Search**:
  - "Search for the latest SpaceX launch"
  - "Who won the Super Bowl?"
- **Smart Answers**:
  - "Who is the CEO of OpenAI?" (Uses LLM + Web Search)
- **Weather**:
  - "What's the weather like?"
  - "Weather in Tokyo"
- **Screen Vision**:
  - "What is on my screen?"
  - "Explain this error message"
  - "Summarize this article"

## 4. System Control
- **App Management**:
  - "Open Chrome"
  - "Open Notepad"
  - "Open Spotify"
  - "Open VS Code"
- **Volume Control**:
  - "Volume up"
  - "Mute"
- **Screenshots**:
  - "Take a screenshot"

## 5. Finance Tracking
- **Log Expenses**:
  - "Spent $50 on groceries"
  - "I bought a coffee for $5"
- **Analyze Spending**:
  - "How much did I spend on food?"
  - "Total spending today"

## 6. Memory & Personalization
- **Long-term Memory**:
  - "Remember that my dog's name is Max"
  - "My favorite color is blue"
- **Recall**:
  - "What is my dog's name?"

## 7. Adaptive Features
- **Noise Calibration**: Automatically adjusts to background noise (fans, AC) to avoid false activations.
- **Context Awareness**: Knows the current date, time, and day of the week to give relevant advice.
